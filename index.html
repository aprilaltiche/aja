Projects
Student Organization Online Registration System - PHP Native


The Student Organization Online Registration System was created for our school’s organization and was presented at ICCT IT/CS day 2019 Booth Exhibit Competition. The system was used by more or less 500 IT students of ICCT Antipolo Campus. This was also purposedly built to resolve the issue of double billing on students. It has a three users, the Administrator, Officers and the Students.


The Students needs to register to the registration page and will receive an email after the successful registration. After the registration students can go to the Org’s officers to pay a registration fee, the officer can now scan the ID of the student where we have barcodes and then the student will receive a receipt on their registered email. A student can only register once.
To avoid the issue of double billing on students, officer’s account can only view students who has paid on their accounts they cannot removed any student on their list.

For the officers to have an account to the system, the system has a registration page for them before they can use and login their account they must be approved by the Administrator.
The Administrator has the ability to remove, add, edit, update an officer’s account and Student’s details.

Therapy tools Inventory and Book management system - VB.NET


The Therapy tools Inventory and Book management system was built for NEST Inc. The system resolved the problem on manual inventory of Therapy tools used by the Clinic for their daily session. Nest has more than 5000 therapy tools being counted every week by the Admin assistant which took weeks to complete but beauase of the system, monitoring of the equipments automated and fasten the process and lessen the use of paper.


Client’s account has a registration an login page as well and subject for approval of the Administrator. Client’s request for helpers can be cancelled as long as the request has not been approved by the Admnitrator.
The Administrator can add, edit, delete, update clients and helper accounts.

Helper Request Ordering System - PHP Native



The Helper Request Ordering System was created as a final thesis requirement. It has a helper,client, and administrator account. The helper has a registration and login page where the helper will select his available time starting from 8am to 5pm. List of available helpers can be seen in Client’s dashboard where they can choose and pick. Client can see the helper’s profile and contact details. After a client choose and request the helper, the Administrator will receive an email notification about the request.


The system can generate a barcode number. It has two users, the Assistant and Admin account, where the assitant’s acount can only update the count of the item and also can add an item. It has a print fucntion for generation of reports.

The Admin account has the ability to add,edit,delete and update an item and generation of reports.
